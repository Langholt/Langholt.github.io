I am Friðrik Lárusson // Ég heiti Friðrik Lárusson

I was born & raised in the Langholt neighborhood in Reykjavík, Iceland // Ísland.

I work as an engineer // verkfræðingur at New Horizons, within Global Good.

Recently I have been working on several projects within global health and development aimed at disease eradication & supporting the global vaccine supply chain. Our two early projects, MetaFridge and Indigo, were introduced at the Goalkeepers event.

I am interested in enabling medicine and healthcare through technology. I co-founded hark which automates and streamlines data capture during doctor-patient encounters.

I earned my doctorate in electrical engineering at Tufts University within the LaISR laboratory led by Prof. Eric L. Miller working with Prof. Sergio Fantini. My work focused primarily on inverse problems, especially image reconstruction methods for diffuse optical tomography.

In a past life, I worked for the hydrological survey of Iceland // Vatnamælingar.
